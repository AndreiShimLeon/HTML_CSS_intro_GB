Keychrone K3 pro

The K3 Max is a low-profile wireless mechanical keyboard with a 75% layout. It supports 2.4 GHz, Bluetooth, and wired connections. Empowered by QMK/VIA, it offers endless possibilities and enhanced productivity in your work and gaming!

Keychrone Q0

The Q0 is a mini custom number pad, it’s designed as a number pad or it can act as an extra key set of macros. With its all-metal CNC machined body, screw-in stabs, hot-swappable function, RGB LEDs, pre-lubed switches, and QMK/VIA support, the Q0 is the best customizable number pad for you.

Keychron Q1 Max

Meet the Keychron Q1 Max, a full metal QMK/VIA wireless custom mechanical keyboard upgraded with a 2.4 GHz wireless connection, featuring a 1000 Hz polling rate. Elevating the typing experience further, it has been thoughtfully upgraded with multiple layers of acoustic foams, ensuring a premium sound quality with every keystroke.
